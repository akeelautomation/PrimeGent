Privacy policy

How PrimeGent handles privacy

This page explains what information the site may collect, how third-party tools can affect visitors, and how PrimeGent handles affiliate-linked content.

Last updated

March 27, 2026.

Overview

PrimeGent is a content-driven men's style website. It publishes articles, curated product picks, and affiliate links. This policy explains what information may be collected through the site and what to update if you add new tools or services later.

Information collected

Because PrimeGent is primarily a static site, it may collect little or no personal information by default. If you add analytics, newsletter tools, contact forms, or other embedded services, those services may collect data such as IP address, browser information, referral source, or submitted contact details.

How information is used

Information may be used to understand site performance, respond to inquiries, improve content, prevent abuse, and manage any newsletter or contact submissions you later add. PrimeGent should only collect data that supports operating and improving the site.

Cookies and analytics

If analytics or advertising tools are installed, those tools may use cookies or similar technologies. Update this page whenever a new analytics platform, consent tool, or tracking script is added so visitors understand what is being measured and why.

Affiliate links and third-party sites

Some PrimeGent links lead to Amazon or other merchants. If a visitor clicks one of those links, the merchant may track the referral and handle any resulting purchase under its own privacy policy and terms. PrimeGent does not control those third-party policies.

Your choices

Visitors can choose not to submit information through email or future forms, and they can review browser settings or extensions that limit cookies and other trackers. If consent tools are added later, this policy should be updated to explain those options clearly.

Contact for privacy questions

For privacy-related questions or requests, use the details on the contact page. If you operate the site publicly, replace any placeholder inboxes with a monitored address before launch.
